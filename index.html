Who am I?

I am a bubbly, passionate, and curious winemaker with a love for Chenin Blanc.

In my free time, I enjoy playing guitar, cooking, painting, and spending time with my manic, orange cats.

What am I doing?

I have an undergraduate degree in Viticulture and Oenology from Stellenbosch University, and a Master's degree from ESA in Angers, France.

I have worked harvests at Tokara and Villiera in Stellenbosch, and Domaine des Baumard in Anjou, France. My next stop will be at Kaapzicht for the 2026 harvest.

I am currently working on a wine under my own label, so watch this space!
